📜 Poems From the Paws

🌸 Romance

The Cat Who Crashed the Call

I hopped upon the laptop keys,
mid-presentation, with such ease,
my human shrieked, the colleagues laughed,
my fluffy rear was autographed.

The meeting froze, the faces stared,
I stretched and yawned, I simply cared
for nothing more than that one patch
of sunlight only I could catch.
🎙️ Comedy

You're Still on Mute

Meow meow MEOW MEOW meow,
(translation: you are still on mute somehow).
I've told you seventeen times today,
click the little mic — okay?

Your coworkers can see me though,
I've made their afternoon a show,
they screenshot me, they share and swoon —
I am the star of your Zoom room.
✨ Mystical

Digital Familiar

I walk through pixels, soft and sleek,
a ghost of fur and polished cheek,
I haunt the borders of your screen,
the softest thing you've ever seen.

By day I guard your bandwidth wide,
by night I sleep curled by your side,
no firewall, no antivirus —
just me, the cat who won't dismiss us.
🌞 Cheerful

Biscuit Time on Zoom

I knead the keyboard left and right,
I make the spreadsheet quite a sight,
the figures dance, the columns sprawl —
a masterpiece! I typed it all.

My paws are soft, my heart is bold,
my whiskers worth their weight in gold,
so boop the screen and say hello —
it's biscuit time on Zoom, let's go!
🔥 Dramatic

Ode to the Ring Light

O glorious ring of holy light,
you make my pupils dilate bright,
I gaze into your glowing halo —
my human yells, "That's for my Cameo!"

But I am art, I am the muse,
the internet can only lose
its heart to me, each clip and frame —
the ring light knew I'd steal the game.
🌙 Dreamy

Night Shift Purring

While your eyes grew tired and dim,
I watched the notification swim,
I batted gently at the light
of every ping that crossed the night.

And when the meeting finally ends,
when silence through the darkness bends,
I'll curl upon your tired chest
and purr you gently into rest.
🍩 Snack Break

The Tuna Agenda

Item one: the tuna bowl.
Item two: reclaim my soul.
Item three: the sunny chair.
Item four: ignore all stares.

This meeting's running long, I say,
my kibble isn't getting any warmer.
I move to adjourn — all in favour? Meow.
Motion carried. See you tomorrow.
😤 Petty

You Looked Away

You looked away for half a breath,
I pushed your coffee to its death.
It tumbled slowly, gloriously down —
you gasped, I watched, I did not drown

in guilt. I never do. I stare
at consequence with zero care.
The mug is gone, the deed is done.
Refill it. I'm not even one
percent sorry. Look at me.
This was inevitable. ☕
🌿 Zen

The Nap Philosopher

To sleep is not to waste the day —
it is to let the day wash away,
to press reset on every thought,
to live the quiet life I sought.

The humans rush with things to do,
I press my nose into my shoe
and breathe, and stretch, and close my eyes.
This is the only exercise
a creature of pure wisdom needs:
a warm soft spot. The universe agrees.
🔮 Cosmic

Staring at the Wall

There is a thing behind the wall.
You cannot see it. I see all.
It hums at frequencies so low
your limited ears can't even know.

I track it with my ancient eyes,
I guard you from its slow disguise.
You call it "nothing." You call me "strange."
I call it Tuesday in my range.
🎭 Dramatic

The Empty Bowl

The bowl is empty. I am slain.
No mortal has known greater pain.
I lay beside the vacant dish
and mourn the ghost of every fish.

You filled it — five whole minutes past!
But time is short and nothing lasts.
A single kibble more, I beg —
I press my face against your leg.

You check: the bowl is full. I know.
The performance was the point. Let's go.
🌱 Wholesome

The Slow Blink

I look at you across the room,
the afternoon a golden bloom,
and slowly, softly close my eyes —
the truest love that never lies.

No words, no grand romantic fuss,
just this small blink that says: it's us.
You blink back slowly. Now we're bound
by something softer than a sound.
🎪 Chaotic

3 AM Zoomies

Three o'clock: the signal fires.
Something in my brain rewires.
I launch myself off every wall,
I thunder down the darkened hall.

I knock the lamp, I flip the rug,
I body-slam the sleeping pug.
And then — as fast as it began —
I sit and lick my paw. Deadpan.

You watched, confused, from your bed frame.
I have no explanation. Same.
💻 Tech

Ctrl + Cat

I closed the tab you needed most,
I sent the email — I'm the host.
I scheduled seven meetings (none
of which make sense) and then I'm done.

Your password's now a string of paws,
your desktop wallpaper: my claws.
The IT team cannot explain
why all your fonts are Comic Sans again.

I did it. I regret nothing.
Your laptop is my everything.
☀️ Morning

Rise and Feed Me

It's 5 AM, the sun's not up,
but I have needs. I sit. Erupt
into a purr beside your ear —
a sound designed for you to hear

even through sleep. I tap your nose.
I sit upon your sleeping toes.
I lick your forehead. You groan "no."
I knock your phone onto the floor.

You rise. You feed me. Golden hour.
You are my favourite superpower.
🌌 Existential

Window Theory

The bird beyond the glass is mocking me.
It exists in a dimension I can't see
through paw-prints on the pane — I've tried,
I've chirped at it, I've pressed and sighed.

The glass is cold but I persist,
a philosopher who can't resist
the question that the bird won't answer:
what if I too were a dancer
in that wide impossible sky?
(I'd probably just nap. But still. Why.)
🎁 Gifted

The Gift I Brought You

I brought you something from outside.
A present wrapped in hunter's pride.
You screamed. I dropped it at your feet
and waited for the thanks to meet

my patient, glowing amber eyes.
You wept. You said you'd said goodbyes
to catching things. I do not care.
My love language: I was there.

The gift was fine. Your reaction: less.
Tomorrow I'll try harder. Bless.
